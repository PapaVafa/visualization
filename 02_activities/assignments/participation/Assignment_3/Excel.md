I used Excel to clean and organize the dataset for further analysis. 
I started by removing columns that contained insufficient or inconsistent data, as they did not offer reliable insights. 
Then, I grouped the schools by city and region to analyze their geographic distribution. 
I also classified the institutions based on the types of programs they offer and other relevant attributes, which helped create a clearer structure for visual exploration and summary in the spreadsheet.
